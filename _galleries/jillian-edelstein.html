Leading London seafood restaurant, J Sheekey Oyster Bar commissioned this series of Fishlove photographs featuring West End theatre actors holding fish against their bare skin to support the campaign against overfishing in British seas. We are calling for greater protection of UK Coastal waters through Marine Protected Areas. For more information, go to the Marine Conservation Society's website www.mcsuk.org
